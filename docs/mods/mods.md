# <b> 6: Game Modifications </b>

Modifications that give the player a massive gameplay advantage are strictly forbidden. Players who are caught using banned modifications in official league matches, official PASS Fortress and or 4v4 PASS Time matches, scrims, PUGs, officials of other leagues and any other matches in a competitive-like setting will be penalised on a case-by-case basis depending on intent and impact of the modification. Possible penalties may include team and player warnings, forfeiting of one or more match rounds or default losses and / or league bans for the offending player, with some modifications leading to cheating bans even if used outside of TF2 competitive-like settings (see below).

<b> Examples of banned modifications: </b>

- Box/Square shadows.
- Surface texture modifications that function like a wallhack.
- Removing map props, or making them transparent to see what is behind said props.
- Oversized healing particles. 

<b> Examples of allowed modifications: </b>

- Custom huds.
- Custom hit/kill sounds.
- Custom viewmodels and crosshairs.
- Custom JACK models and JACK crosshairs.
- Removing soundscapes.
- Flat textures.
- No-hats mods
- Transparent viewmodels (Only in combination with r_drawviewmodel 1 to avoid map props)
- Changing the color of overheal particles, without changing their position or size.
- Switching or changing sounds of grenade or rocket launchers.

Any mods not listed here should be considered <b>use at your own risk</b> and could result in a ban if they are deemed to provide an unfair advantage. If you are unsure about the legality of your mod, please reach out to an admin of PASS Fortress or your tournament organizer.
For contacting PASS Fortress admins, please join the <a href=https://discord.gg/CtGkSQcRzt>PASS Fortress discord</a> and create a modmail ticket.
