# 1.1: Any Form of Harassment/Racism/Toxicity/Etc is Punishable​

We do not support any form of harassment of its players. People harassing others based on ethnicity, culture, religion or similar examples will get punished for their actions. We understand that things can get heated within a competitive game and we handle cases with this in mind. But there is a fine line between shit talking competition wise and blatantly being an asshole.
We ask you to remain as civil as possible when it comes to situations like this and to treat others with respect.
