    5: Scripts

    Scripts are used to make certain player actions easier or similar, scripts that are used to disrupt gameplay by making the player harder to hit by glitching or freezing the playermodel are banned.

    Usage of banned scripts will be penalized on a case-on-case basis, depending on the intent of the usage of the script. Possible penalties may include: team warnings, forfeiting one or more rounds of a match, and or future tournament bans.

    Examples of banned scripts:

    • Any way to achieve greater turn angles with Demoman's shields.*
    • Freeze glitching.
    • Using the contracker / "IPad" with the intent to disrupt the JACK's behavior. (including using the in game buttons)
    • Any way of bypassing the in-game wait command by using external programs to time inputs for the player.

    *In PASS Fortress every player has access to the same turn angles, regardless of fps or mouse filter settings, however in TF2's 4v4 PASS Time this is unfixed. Because of this we allow "fps_max 60" and "m_filter 1" in tournaments played in TF2, any other way to achieve greater turn angles is not allowed.

    Examples of allowed scripts:

    • Duck jump script.
    • 1-Tick C-tap scripts.
    • C-tap scripts.
    • Jump bug scripts.
    • Scripts that allow fps to be changed for Demoman Shield Charges.
    • Null canceling movement script (Including snap tap keyboards)
    • Using a controller (Including analog movement scripts)
    • Ubercharge mask script.
    • Medic radar script.
    • Team say scripts.
    • Resupply and changing loadout scripts.
    • Viewmodel and crosshair scripts.

    Any scripts not listed here should be considered use at your own risk and could result in a ban if they are deemed to provide an unfair advantage. If you are unsure about the legality of your script, please reach out to an admin of PASS Fortress or your tournament organizer. For contacting PASS Fortress admins, please join the PASS Fortress discord and create a modmail ticket.
